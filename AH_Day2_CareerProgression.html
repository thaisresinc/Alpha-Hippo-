Your Next Role
What Mini Team Leader actually means
🏆
MINI TEAM LEADER / SENIOR SETTER
Still on the phones. Now building a team too.
This isn't a management desk job. You stay on the phones — keeping your own numbers sharp — while taking on responsibility for recruiting, training, and running a small team of up to 5 new starters. You lead by example because you're still doing the job yourself.
On the phones
You continue dialling, hitting your own KPIs, and modelling the standard for your team every single shift.
Recruiting
Helping identify and bring in new potential starters — building the pipeline of talent for the team.
Training
Running onboarding and call coaching for new starters — sharing what you've learned, the right way.
Running a team
Managing up to 5 people — their performance, their development, their accountability. Small team, real responsibility.
📅
WHEN THIS CONVERSATION HAPPENS
After 12 months of succeeding in your current role — not just surviving it. This is discussed with your TL when you've shown sustained performance, leadership qualities, and the kind of character that earns trust. You don't apply for it. You demonstrate it.
