LEAD SOURCE
Alpha Hippo's door knockers feed your team — not Jordan's
Alpha Hippo has its own door knocking team (~20–25 people), separate from RESINC's. Alpha Hippo door knockers → your team. RESINC door knockers → Jordan's team, which also receives online enquiries and other mixed-quality channels.
WHY YOUR LEADS ARE DIFFERENT
Every lead has already been spoken to face-to-face
No cold internet leads. No mixed sources. Every lead you work has already had a real conversation with one of our own door knockers and showed enough interest to book. That's your advantage on every call.
