TOUR STEP 1 OF 6
Welcome to your dashboard
This is what you'll open at the start of every single shift. The green LIVE indicator in the top right means the data is real-time — it updates as calls happen. Click ↺ Refresh at any time to pull the latest numbers.
1 / 6
TOUR STEP 2 OF 6
Two views — Weekly Breakdown & Daily Monitor
Weekly Breakdown is your primary view. All your KPIs for the current week, with comparison to last week. This is where you check progress.

Daily Monitor shows a day-by-day breakdown. Green = improvement vs previous day. Red = a drop. Use this to catch dips before they become a bad week. Try clicking the Daily Monitor tab above.
2 / 6
TOUR STEP 3 OF 6
Reading the colour codes
Every cell in the Daily Monitor is colour coded against the previous day. You don't need to calculate anything — just look for red. Wednesday shows a dip in dials (51 vs 64 on Tuesday). That's the signal to ask: what happened Wednesday? Was it a later start? Fewer new leads? Catch it on Thursday and adjust — don't wait until the end of the week.
3 / 6
TOUR STEP 4 OF 6
Filtering — All Reps vs My Performance
The filter row lets you toggle between All Reps (team view) and My Performance (your numbers only). Use team view to understand where you sit relative to your teammates. Use personal view when you're doing a self-review or prep for a coaching session. Try clicking "My Performance" above.
4 / 6
TOUR STEP 5 OF 6
Reading the numbers — the performance funnel
Work through the metrics left to right, top to bottom — they follow a funnel. Active Hrs → Dials → Pick-Ups → Convos → Bookings → Sat → Sales. When something drops, trace it upstream. Bookings low? Check Pick-Ups. Pick-Ups low? Check Dials. Dials low? Check Active Hours. Find the break in the funnel.
5 / 6
TOUR STEP 6 OF 6 — COMPLETE
Your dashboard is a mirror ✓
What you put in is exactly what it shows back. Low numbers on the dashboard always mean the same thing — low activity somewhere upstream. The data doesn't lie, and it doesn't judge. It just shows you where to focus. Use it every single day — not just when things are going wrong.

YOUR PROGRESSION
What good looks like at 30, 60 & 90 days

These aren't arbitrary targets — they're the benchmarks Luke uses to track your development. Know where you're heading before you get there.

MILESTONE
30 Days
  • →Displaying all core values on and off the phone
  • →Active in the GC — sharing calls for feedback with peers and Luke
  • →Call efficiency above 40%
  • →Close rate 10%, pushing toward 20% as weeks go on
  • →Full 25 active hour weeks by week 3–4
  • →Call scores above 5/10, covering the Big 5
MILESTONE
60 Days
  • →Efficiency 45%+
  • →20 bookings per week
  • →Turning "not interested" into booked appointments
  • →Net sit rate 33%+
  • →Communicating USPs and solar concepts clearly with conviction
  • →Independently working — reaching out for feedback proactively
MILESTONE
90 Days
  • →Exceeding all KPIs consistently
  • →Efficiency 50%, active hours 30+, close rate 25%+
  • →125+ pick-ups per week
  • →Contributing to the team — sharing feedback and call reviews
  • →Self-developing in own time, bringing ideas to the team
  • →Spreading positivity — communicative and present every day
