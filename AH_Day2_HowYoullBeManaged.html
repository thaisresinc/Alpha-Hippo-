Section 1
Monthly 1-1s
📊
FORMAL REVIEW
Monthly — 1 hour blocked off
FORMAT
One hour, one-on-one with Luke. Scheduled monthly. Bring your calls and your questions.
FIRST 30 DAYS
Focus is on enthusiasm and willingness to learn. After 30 days, Luke reviews the data and plans your development from there.
AGENDA — EVERY 1-1
  • Last month's stats — reviewed together against targets
  • What's going well — named and acknowledged
  • Focus areas — where to improve and how
  • Plan and set targets for the month ahead
  • Agreed actions sent in writing via email — keeps accountability clear
  • Your chance to raise concerns or ask for feedback on anything
Section 2
Team Meetings & Check-ins
📋
Weekly
Weekly Summary
Review the week's results and set targets for the week ahead. Full team. Come with your numbers.
Mondays · 7:30am · 30 mins
⚡
Daily — 3×
Daily Check-ins
Sprint reviews, roleplays, call reviews, updates, and focus areas. Short, sharp, and purposeful.
7:30am · 10:15am · 1:00pm
🎙️
Regular
Call Reviews
3 calls reviewed per session — listened as a team. Feedback shared individually and live. Recordings stored on the dialler.
As part of daily check-ins
🎙️ HOW CALL REVIEWS WORK
👥
Team listens together
3 calls played to the whole team. Everyone hears, everyone learns.
💬
Feedback — group & individual
Group discussion on the call, plus individual feedback from Luke via DM or Zoom.
📞
Live feedback too
Especially at the start — Luke jumps on live calls to coach in real time.
✅
YOUR RESPONSIBILITY
Have calls ready for review. Don't wait to be asked — share proactively with your TL. The setters who share calls improve the fastest. Make it a daily habit, not a reaction.
Section 3
Support During a Shift
💬
PRIMARY CHANNEL
Google Chat
For questions, live feedback, and anything that comes up mid-shift. Post in the group — your teammates can help too. Don't suffer in silence between calls.
💬 Google Chat — group or DM
📲
URGENT ONLY
WhatsApp Call
For genuinely urgent situations that can't wait for a Chat message. Reserve this for when it's actually urgent — not every question.
📞 WhatsApp call → TL
🎯
LIVE CALL FEEDBACK
Real-time coaching
Post in the Google Chat group during a live call if you need help. Teammates and Luke can feed you language in real time — use the team around you.
💬 Google Chat group
⬆️
ESCALATION
When Luke needs to escalate
If something is outside Luke's remit, he'll escalate to senior management via Zoho tag, chat, or email. You don't need to go above Luke directly.
Luke → Zoho tag / email
