WHAT THIS MEANS IN PRACTICE
When it's 7:30am in Bali, it's already 9:30am in Brisbane. Your TL and the AU team are already at their desks. Plan your day with this in mind — especially for callbacks and anything that requires TL support.
DAYLIGHT SAVING
Australia observes daylight saving from October to April. During this period, Brisbane moves to AEDT (UTC+11) — the gap increases from 2 hours to 3 hours. The schedule stays the same — Bali absorbs the change.
🌏
SCHEDULE STABILITY
The schedule stays the same during daylight saving. It can be subject to change based on contact % — Luke will communicate any adjustments in advance.
Section 3
Running Late or Calling in Sick
⏰
Running Late
WhatsApp text to Luke as soon as you know. Don't wait until your start time to let him know — notify ASAP the moment you realise you'll be late. Include an estimated arrival time.
📱 WhatsApp text → TL
🤒
Calling in Sick
The night before if you know, or first thing in the morning if you wake up unwell. Contact by WhatsApp call — not a text, not a message in the group chat. Call Luke directly.
📞 WhatsApp call → TL
Section 4
Public Holidays
🇦🇺
Australian Public Holidays
The Bali team follows NSW and ACT public holidays. On these days, the team is offline. Lead volume will be lower and dialling windows may shift — customers in those states are less available.
🇮🇩
Balinese Public Holidays
Balinese public holidays are not observed as standard rest days for the team. If you have a specific holiday you need to take, discuss it with Luke in advance — standard leave protocols apply.
